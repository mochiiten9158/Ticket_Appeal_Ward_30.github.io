Every year, the city's ticketing system brings in millions of dollars in revenue with a disproportionate emphasis on African-Americans.


These tickets can be appealed. We can help!


A team ProPublica and WBEZ found out that in the process of acquiring over $200 million every year in ticketing revenue, Chicago enforcement disproportionately affects black drivers. People who already cannot afford to meet the requirements are inevitably saddled with additional fees and charges, which can even spiral into bankruptcy or vehicle impoundment.
